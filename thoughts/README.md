1. on Measuring Progress
To meausure progress you need to first be able to register it.
- When was the first time I registered progress? 
Progress only registers to myself when it is the result of deliberate effort in some
direction. When I was in my early teens, the direction was decided by me being a part of the competitive squash circuit. Registering progress is exhilarating. There was an almost visceral reaction beating a guy that seemed untouchable at the time. In contrast, during the same period I never felt any sense of progress from my academic achievement - and that was just due to how dominant I was compared to everyone around me - just due to my innate abiliity. 
- How to register progress
It seems like registering progress requires: 
1. Having a goal that requires your directed effort.
2. Having multiple instances of similar situations where you apply that directed effort. Its very likely that you'd have to create these situations yourself, cannot expect them to be around all the time.
